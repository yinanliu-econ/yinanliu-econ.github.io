Yinan Liu (刘逸楠)

Assistant Professor, School of Applied Economics, Renmin University of China

My research interests primarily lie in **Public Economics**, **Health Economics**, **Development Economics**, and **Applied Microeconomics**. I am particularly interested in understanding the economic impacts of public policies and technological advancements on health outcomes and economic development, often utilizing micro-econometric methods.
